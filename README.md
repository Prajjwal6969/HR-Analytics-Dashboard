# HR Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This HR Analytics Dashboard helps analyze employee attrition, workforce trends, salary distribution, and employee satisfaction using interactive visualizations.

The dashboard provides meaningful insights that can help HR teams improve employee retention and organizational performance.

---

## 📊 Key Metrics

- Total Employees: 1470
- Attrition Count: 237
- Attrition Rate: 16%
- Average Salary: 6.5K
- Average Working Years: 7 Years

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

### 1. Highest Attrition in Age Group 26–35
Employees aged between 26–35 years have the highest attrition count.

### 2. Salary Impacts Retention
Most employees leaving the organization belong to the salary slab below 5K.

### 3. Department-wise Attrition
Laboratory Technicians and Sales Executives show the highest attrition.

### 4. Education Field Analysis
Life Sciences and Medical fields contribute the highest employee turnover.

### 5. Early Career Attrition
Employees are more likely to leave during their initial years at the company.
